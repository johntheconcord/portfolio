Snake Grow

1. Welcome to Snake Grow! Instructions easy here, just make your snake grow by getting your white snake you eat as many red blocks as possible.

2. You control your snake by with the LEFT, RIGHT, UP, and DOWN arrows on your keyboard. Going backwards, the opposite direction, or running into your snake tail will make you lose all your snake growth so be careful!

3. You lose all your snake growth by running into any of the walls. Don't worry, you can just start right up again.

4. Enjoy!
